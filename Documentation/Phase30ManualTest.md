2. With an available Wildflower, Young Tree, or Stone Outcrop, observe the compact green guide pointer/light while Mossling leads. The existing target marker/trail still identifies the resource.

5. Move away from Mossling while no guide target is active to observe its small follow leaf; RESET/unbefriended state has only the idle mote.
6. Restart after befriending and after a renewal state; confirm the existing companion persistence, guide target selection, and RESET behavior are unchanged.
