# Phase 21 Manual Test — Dodge and Combat Readability

1. Open `Assets/Game/Scenes/Bootstrap.unity`, enter Play mode, and use **RESET** for a clean route.
2. Move with WASD/arrow keys, then press **Left Shift**. Confirm a short directional dodge burst, warm motion trail, and compact cooldown feedback.
3. Repeat with the gamepad east face button and the landscape-safe **DODGE** touch button. All three inputs must invoke the same action.
4. Let the practice slime approach. Confirm its pre-contact pulse/anticipation is visible and audible before normal contact damage.
5. Dodge through contact during the short active window and confirm no contact damage; wait for dodge completion/cooldown and confirm ordinary contact damage still applies.
6. Restore Wayroot and approach the Thorn Guardian. Confirm its stronger anticipation is visible/audible before contact, then repeat the dodge check.
7. Verify **HOLD ATTACK**, **HOLD GATHER**, pause, shelter return, Mossling guide, Journey guidance, restart, and RESET remain usable.

This remains desktop review evidence, not physical-iPhone validation.
