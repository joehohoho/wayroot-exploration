# Phase 31 Manual Test — Combat Feel and Environmental Ambience

1. Start from **RESET**. Confirm **SOUND ON** is retained and the quiet original ambient bed is audible. Toggle SOUND OFF/ON; all Phase 31 cues must respect the existing saved preference.
2. In Sunmeadow, move beside the existing Practice Slime and hold **ATTACK / SPACE**. Confirm compact contact flash/trail, short original contact tone, and optional haptic seam only; attack range, cooldown, damage, core award, and controls are unchanged.
3. Let the Slime or Guardian make contact. Confirm the established anticipation signal remains readable, a short player-damage tone is heard for non-lethal contact, dodge immunity remains unchanged, and defeat still restores health at the existing default/shelter destination with its existing UI feedback.
4. Defeat an enemy and wait for its existing respawn duration. Confirm its established defeat/respawn visuals remain compact, a restrained procedural respawn tone plays, and rewards/respawn timing are unchanged.
5. Observe the route without moving. Confirm small creek ripples, leaf sway, and sparse motes enrich Sunmeadow. Visit/unlock the existing Grove, Glade, shelter, and Bloomwell states to see only their small, landmark-bound ambient mote; effects must not cover actors, HUD, or touch controls.
6. Restart after toggling SOUND and after any combat/resource progress. Confirm the existing save, RESET, route gates, resources, values, and persistence behavior remain unchanged.

Desktop review is not physical-iPhone validation. The haptic seam intentionally has no platform plug-in in this Windows-safe prototype build.
