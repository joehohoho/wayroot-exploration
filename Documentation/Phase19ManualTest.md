# Phase 19 Manual Test — Bloomwell Restoration Finale

## Purpose

Review the complete compact completion arc using only existing interactions and renewable materials. This is a playable finale state, not a credits sequence or a new region/system.

## Start and controls

1. Open `Assets/Game/Scenes/Bootstrap.unity` and enter Play mode, or launch `Builds/Phase19Review/WayrootPhase19.exe`.
2. Select **RESET** for a fresh route. Use WASD/arrow keys or MOVE; **hold E / HOLD GATHER** (gamepad south / touch GATHER) to interact; hold Space / ATTACK to fight.

## Complete route

1. Gather renewable WILDFLOWER, YOUNG TREE, and STONE OUTCROP materials; defeat Slimes for cores. Build/rest at shelter and purchase Iron Edge as required by the existing Wayroot route.
2. Restore the Wayroot, defeat Thorn Guardian, then follow the open violet path into Moonlit Glade.
3. Gather the existing glade/Sunmeadow renewable nodes until inventory has **2 PETAL + 2 TIMBER + 2 STONE + 1 CORE**.
4. At **BLOOMWELL — HOLD GATHER**, activate it. Confirm the bundle is consumed exactly once; the action card says `BLOOMWELL RESTORED — SUNMEADOW BLOOMS ANEW`; the completion line becomes visible; and the original magical restoration cue plays when SOUND is on.
5. Confirm the Bloomwell heart/petals/light, Sunmeadow finale blooms and warmer-violet light, and Mossling’s celebration response are all visible. Gathering, renewal, companion guide, combat, shelter, Wayroot, guardian, and glade remain playable.

## Missing-requirement, persistence, and reset checks

- Try Bloomwell early: feedback names each missing existing item (PETAL, TIMBER, STONE, CORE), with no new resource/currency/crafting requirement.
- Restart after completion: restored Bloomwell label/visual, Sunmeadow motif/light, Mossling response, and compact `FINALE COMPLETE` HUD state remain.
- Select RESET: all progress including Bloomwell completion clears; the glade seals again and the Sunmeadow motif is absent on the fresh load.

## Scope note

Desktop validation only; it does not replace the separately blocked physical-iPhone validation.
